A Tribute for Teresita P. Tan

For those who don't know me, my name is Shaira Montojo, and I am Teresita Tan's granddaughter.
On behalf of my grandmother, my parents and the rest of our family, I want to thank you for visiting
this page that I made for my grandmother.

My grandmother lived a life that was full of joy, and happiness. whenever I think about her,
I remember how humorous, witty, and clever she was. Most of my childhood memories with her
are full with traces of singing and laughter. It was so much fun to be with her, she loved to laugh and to
have fun, we adored her.

She faced her life with intelligencem courage, and wit. When I was talking to some of her friends, I
heard more than anything else was how much fun she was to be around. We all remember how
lighthearted she was and how much she liked to enjoy herself.

Lola, I will always miss you. But I have so many wonderful memories of you. I'll always think of you with a smile.
